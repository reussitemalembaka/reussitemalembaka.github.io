Reussite B. Malembaka

I am an agricultural scientist, interested in agroecology, soil science, and food systems ecological impacts assessment

My PhD research is part of the DELIBERATE project, a trans-disciplinary project that seeks to answer to the question : Does food democracy matters?. The project aims to understand whether and how the deliberative quality in soybean and coffee value chains influence their power relations and ecological 'foodprints.'

Under the guidance of Prof. Johanna Jacobi and Stephan Pfister, I've been focusing on the sustainability analysis of soybean production and value chains. I am curious to assess the environmental impacts of different soybean production systems (maintream and alternatives) in Brazil and the Democratic Republic of Congo, and to also evaluate the poultry linked value chains from Brazil, to local and global supply. I am using the life cycle assessment to answer these questions, with field data collections, advanced methods and tools of regionalized LCIA and relevant impacts categories such as Greenhouse gases emissions, land use , biodiversity loss, agrobiodiversity, soil quality, etc.
