    DELIERATE project : Linking deliberative quality of food systems to their ecological footprints

    This project focused in the tropics : Latin America and Africa, and focused on key commodities crops: Coffee and soybean
    We explored the conventional and alternative systems of entire agri-food value chains and assessed the related socio-ecological footprints

    CO2 compensation - agroforestry action research project, in 2024 and 2025

    Promotion and implementation of Agroforestry systems in soybean and coffee small-scale farming in DR Congo.
    As an action research project, we carried out activities of training farmers on Agroecology, trees seedlings production (distribution to farmers through cooperatives), erosion control and integration tree-coffee soybean.
    The project was supported by the ETH -DUSYS CO" compensation Fund.
